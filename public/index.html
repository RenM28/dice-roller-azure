Left, Right, Center Dice Roller

Instructions:

If you're dying to play some good old fashioned LRC but don't have any dice lying around, today's your lucky day!
Simply click the roll button below or hit enter to roll all 3 dice. True to the game, you can either roll a "L", "R", "C", or ".".
If you get an L, you pass to the left, an R means pass to the right, C means you pass to the center, and . means you're safe.
Have fun and roll away!
